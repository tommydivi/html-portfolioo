The Best Movies According to Tom Diviney

My top 5 movies of all-time.

Goodfellas

A timeless classic and endlessly rewatchable. De Niro on top form, and Pesci playing a despicable, yet hilarious mobster loon.

One Flew Over The Cuckoos Nest

Hilarious and forever quotable. The launch-pad of many well-known actors and home to possibly the most evil villain in movie history - Nurse Ratchett.

This Is Spinal Tap

One of the funniest movies of all time. Stands the test of time and inspired the pseudo-documentary style of film making. This movie dials it up to 11.
